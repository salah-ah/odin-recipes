Prime Rib

Description

Prime rib is a cut of beef from the rib section of a cow. It is usually roasted, but can also be grilled or barbecued. It is sometimes called standing rib roast, because the rib bones are left in the meat.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound) standing rib roast
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  • Place roast in a roasting pan, and rub with olive oil.
  • pour over roast, Cover with aluminum foil.
  • Combine soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, brown sugar
  • Roast in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  • Reduce heat to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C)
  • Remove roast from oven, and let stand for 15 to 20 minutes before carving.
